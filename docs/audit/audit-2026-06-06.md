## Recommendations

### Immediate (no blockers)

Automated suite is green; no fixes required for CI/build health.

1. **Align `goToNextSection` with `selectSection`** — reuse shared navigation cleanup to avoid stale review UI and wrong AI scope/tier.

6. **Localize extract headings** — wire `componentTranslations` into Therapie extract path.
